I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Environmental Engineering under the advisement of Professor Shaily Mahendra at University of California, Los Angeles. Having worked at Professor Lisa Alvarez-Cohen's group, I received my M.S. from University of California at Berkeley in 2019. In 2018, I received my Bachelor’s Degree in Environmental Engineering from Harbin Insitute of Technology (HIT). During my undergraduate, I published a first-author paper with Professor Shijie You and a third-author paper with Professor Nanqi Ren. I was a member of Professor Nanqi Ren's group since my sophomore year, 2015, and won several national awards under his supervision.

My research areas lie in vault nanoparticles packaged enzymes in biordegradation and detoxification, focusing on per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFASs) and other emerging contaminants. I am also interested in mycoremediation and fungal enzymatic bioremediation. Moreover, I am also studying environmental microbial communities and metabolic transformation flux during biodegradation process.
